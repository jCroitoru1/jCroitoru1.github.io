About Me

I like the part between "interesting dataset" and "clear takeaway."

My work sits at the intersection of analysis and communication. I enjoy exploring raw data, finding the signal worth sharing, and packaging the result into something that feels useful, polished, and memorable.

This portfolio highlights that approach through hands-on projects: one focused on music market-style insights, one focused on education analytics, and one focused on presenting my work with clarity.

How I Work

A lightweight process that keeps projects grounded in outcomes.

01

Collect

Start with live or exported data, then identify what is trustworthy, what is noisy, and what is worth keeping.

02

Shape

Turn raw files into a clean structure that supports comparisons, summaries, and fast explanation.

03

Present

Build interfaces and written context that make the analysis understandable to both technical and non-technical audiences.
