About Me

I am interested in the process of turning raw information into clear analysis.

I am most interested in the stage of a project where a messy dataset begins to reveal something meaningful. I enjoy exploring, cleaning, and organizing information until the larger story becomes easier to communicate.

The projects on this site reflect that approach. Some are more exploratory, some focus more on prediction, and others emphasize presenting technical work in a way that is accessible and well structured.

How I Work

My process is straightforward and focused on clarity.

01

Collect

I start by gathering the data and identifying what is usable, what needs refinement, and what is likely noise.

02

Shape

I then organize it into a cleaner structure so that comparisons, summaries, and patterns are easier to evaluate.

03

Present

Finally, I present the work in a way that is understandable to both technical and non-technical audiences.
